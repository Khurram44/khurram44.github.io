The problem

Manual systems make revenue and attendance difficult to control.

In many gyms, attendance, fee reminders, member renewals, and follow-ups depend on staff memory. As the branch gets busy, missed entries and unpaid renewals become hard to catch.

₨

Renewals get delayed

Members often continue after expiry because staff has to manually remember due dates and follow-ups.

!

Attendance is not live

Owners usually find out late when members become irregular or stop visiting the gym.

✓

Identity is weakly checked

Manual systems cannot reliably catch unknown visitors, wrong check-ins, or doubtful attendance records.

GymSecure is built to reduce missed renewals, unreliable attendance, and silent member drop-off.

The solution

AI attendance connected with automated WhatsApp reminders.

GymSecure recognizes members as they enter, marks verified attendance, highlights suspicious detections, and sends timely WhatsApp messages for renewals and member engagement.

1

3

4

WhatsApp follows up

GymSecure does not only record attendance. It turns attendance into business action.
